Spa Maintenance

Regular Spa Cleaning & Care

A well-maintained spa is a safe spa. Improper water chemistry in a hot tub is more than just an inconvenience — it's a health risk. Our regular maintenance service keeps your water balanced and your equipment performing at its best.

  • Complete water chemistry testing and balancing
  • Interior surface cleaning
  • Filter inspection and cleaning
  • Equipment and jet inspection
  • Cover and cabinet inspection
  • Residential, rental, and commercial schedules available

Spa Repair

Fast, Reliable Spa Repair

We stock almost every part you need on our trucks. That means we diagnose and fix most spa problems in a single visit — no waiting days for parts to arrive. We service all major brands.

  • Heater repair and replacement
  • Pump and motor service
  • Jet replacement and repair
  • Control board and topside panel diagnostics
  • Leak detection and repair
  • Plumbing and fitting replacement
  • Vinyl shell repair

Complete Spa Installation

From Delivery to First Soak

Adding a hot tub or spa to your property is a smart investment — especially for vacation rentals on the Outer Banks where guests expect resort-level amenities. We handle the full installation process from delivery coordination to final setup.

  • Site assessment and preparation guidance
  • Safe transport and delivery coordination using our Spa Dolly system
  • Electrical hookup coordination (we work with licensed electricians)
  • Initial fill, chemistry setup, and equipment test
  • Walkthrough and owner education
  • Ongoing maintenance packages available post-install
