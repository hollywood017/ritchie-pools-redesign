Pool Resurfacing

ecoFinish Pool Resurfacing

Ritchie Pools & Spas is proud to offer ecoFinish — the industry-leading aquaBRIGHT pool finish. ecoFinish is a spray-applied, polymer-based coating that bonds directly to your pool surface, delivering a finish that is smoother, more durable, and longer-lasting than traditional plaster or paint.

Unlike old-school resurfacing, ecoFinish doesn't require draining curing time and is resistant to algae, staining, and chemical damage. The result is a stunning, like-new finish that holds up to the demands of the OBX environment — salt air, sun, and heavy use.

  • aquaBRIGHT ecoFinish spray application
  • Bonds to existing surfaces — no tear-out required
  • Algae, stain, and UV resistant
  • Available in multiple colors and finishes
  • Faster cure time than traditional plaster
  • Surface inspection and prep included

Leak Detection & Repair

Find It Fast. Fix It Right.

Losing water and can't figure out why? Pool leaks are more common than most owners realize — and more expensive the longer they go unaddressed. Our technicians use proven methods to locate even the most elusive leaks.
